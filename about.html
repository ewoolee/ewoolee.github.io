About

I am a Korean transplant living in the San Francisco Bay Area since 2007. I studied at UC Berkeley, where I found value in being an interdisciplinary designer through combining my background in cogninive science and human-centered design. In my free time, I like to dance (mostly urban, but I also like trying contemporary) and play the viola.

I am looking for full-time opportunities, but am also open to volunteer/freelance work! I've learned a lot by coding this website myself, so talk to me about if you have a static website in mind. Please don't hesitate to say hi at hello@helenl.ee!
